My Projects

Occuvision

Occuvision is a computer vision application designed to alert restaurant staff when dishes have been left in the kitchen window for too long. Powered by the YOLOv8 object detection model, Occuvision monitors window activity and tracks key metrics such as the rate at which dishes enter and exit. This helps restaurants improve food delivery efficiency and better serve their customers.

Carrie's Closet App

The Carrie's Closet app is a mobile platform developed for a non-profit in the Metro-Atlanta area. It streamlines donation requests of clothing and hygiene items, allowing users to easily submit, track, and manage their requests, while offering employees enhanced oversight in handling these requests. The app centralizes donation activities, making the process more efficient for both users and employees.

Tater Tower

Tater Tower is a potato-themed tower defense game featuring three levels of increasing difficulty. As players progress, they face waves of new enemies, while defending with various types of game potatoes, each with unique damage capabilities and costs. The challenge ramps up as players strategically place their potatoes to fend off increasingly tough enemies and protect their base.

Card Games

This project is a web application that utilizes a card API, allowing users to play Blackjack or War against a computer. Players are required to log in or create a profile, which enables the tracking of game statistics, including wins, losses, and overall performance across the different games. This feature enhances user engagement and offers a personalized gaming experience, making it easy for players to monitor their progress and skills.
